# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not individuals.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abusive language, or discrimination.
- Sharing private credentials or sensitive data in issues/PRs.
- Disruptive behavior that blocks collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
