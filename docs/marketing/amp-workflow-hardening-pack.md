- amp_install_doc_clicks
- amp_setup_guide_clicks

- Do not claim a dedicated Amp marketplace, hosted guide, installs, or revenue without direct command evidence.

- days_0_30: Keep the repo-backed Amp install doc, setup guide follow-on, and outreach copy aligned around one blocked repeat. Decision rule: Do not expand the Amp lane into a bigger launch story unless install-path clicks or conversations show real paid intent.
