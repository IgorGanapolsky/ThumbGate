# Marketing Strategy: Flow Preservation Layer

**Thesis:** Pre-Action Gates are the flow-preservation layer for AI coding. They eliminate the #1 flow-killer: your agent repeating mistakes you already fixed.

## Neuroscience Angle
Reframing the product from a "dev tool" to a "performance tool" using Steven Kotler's Flow Research Collective (FRC) frameworks.

### The 4 Flow Triggers (Mapped to Gates)
1. **Clear Goals:** Pre-Action Gates ensure the agent stays within architectural boundaries, preventing "mission creep" that breaks human clarity.
2. **Immediate Feedback:** When an agent attempts a violation, the gate blocks it *at compute speed*, providing instant corrective feedback before the developer's state is interrupted.
3. **Challenge/Skills Balance:** By handling the "babysitting" of known failures, gates allow the developer to focus on the high-skill/high-challenge parts of the build.
4. **Risk:** Pre-Action Gates treat policy violations as high-cost moves, mirroring the "Risk" trigger identified in FRC's 2026 research.

## Positioning

- **Tagline:** "Stop babysitting. Start flowing."
- **Core Message:** "Your AI Agent is Destroying Your Flow State. And neuroscience explains exactly why..."
