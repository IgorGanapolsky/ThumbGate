- Keep pricing aligned with COMMERCIAL_TRUTH.md.

- days_61_90: Decide whether OpenCode stays a self-serve lane or earns more direct founder-led outbound. Decision rule: Only increase OpenCode-specific outbound after there is qualified conversation or paid-intent evidence.

- guide views without proof clicks
