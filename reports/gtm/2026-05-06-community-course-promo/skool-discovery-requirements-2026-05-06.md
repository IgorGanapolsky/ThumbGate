## Minimum threshold (eligibility checklist)

Skool treats new groups as `UNLISTED` until they meet a minimum threshold.

Checklist (from official docs):

- Cover image set
- Group description added
- About page completed (copy + images/videos)
- At least one post written
- Invite members (and reach minimum threshold of members/posts/activity)

## Ranking levers (once eligible)

Skool’s Discovery ranking algorithm emphasizes:

- Member growth
- Member activity (posts/comments/likes)
- Retention

Skool states there is a human-in-the-loop review for rankings and may apply boosts/penalties.

## Boosts / penalties (qualitative)

Boost examples called out by Skool:

- High-quality artwork / About page
- Interesting niche
- Authentic human engagement
- Active owner/admins

Penalty examples called out by Skool:

- Bots or fake accounts
- Spam / low-quality engagement / points farming
- Low-quality artwork / About page
- Payments off-platform
- Bad customer support
- Inactive owner
